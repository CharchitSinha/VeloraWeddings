How It Started

Built from a
Real Wedding Story

Velora was born out of frustration. Our founders, planning their own wedding in 2019, spent months scouring multiple apps, calling hundreds of vendors, comparing scattered spreadsheets, and losing track of everything. There had to be a better way.

They built Velora — a single, elegant marketplace where couples could discover, compare, and book every wedding vendor they needed, all in one beautifully designed platform.

Today, Velora connects 12,000+ verified vendors with nearly 100,000 couples across 350+ Indian cities. From intimate garden ceremonies to grand palace weddings, every couple deserves a seamless, joyful planning experience.

Our mission is simple: make your wedding as magical to plan as it is to live.
